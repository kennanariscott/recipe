Ingredients


  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 TB salt
  • 4 TB cocoa
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 1 3/4 cups butter
  • 10 TB milk
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup butter milk
  • 2 large beaten eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 3 cups confectioners sugar

Baking the Cake


1. Mix the flour, sugar, and salt together.

2. Put the butter in a pot, and mix the butter, cocoa and 1 cup of boiling water.

3. In a big measuring cup, mix the eggs, baking soda and vanilla.

4. Combine both of the mixes and mix slowly, pour in a pan afterwards.

Making the Frosting

  1. Melt the butter and add cocoa and stir, take off heat.
  2. Add milk, vanilla, sugar and mix then pour on the warm cake
